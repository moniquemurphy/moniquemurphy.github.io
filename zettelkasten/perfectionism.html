--- layout: zettelpage title: "Perfectionism is at heart a fear of mortality" permalink: /perfectionism/ ---

You need time to do things wrong and get better gradually. When you fear running out of time, you need to get it right and right away, because there is no time to revise, no time to improve, and there is always something else demanding one's attention.

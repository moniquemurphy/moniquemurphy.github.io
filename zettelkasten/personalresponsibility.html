--- layout: zettelpage title: "Personal responsibility rhetoric is a fear response" permalink: /personalresponsibility/ ---

Believing that one is in complete control of one's destiny mitigates the fear of the chaos and randomness of the universe. If you can convince yourself that bad things only happen to people who deserve it, and cling to that belief, then you free yourself of the fear of bad things happening to you. It's a form of denial that is useful.

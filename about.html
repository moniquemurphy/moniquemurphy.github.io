About

This is mostly a blog focused on language learning, linguistics, Japanese culture, comics/manga, and other things that strike my fancy. I'm a programmer by day but it's my job and not my life, Sometimes I feel compelled to write about things that were tricky or that I've only seen written about with poor grammar and unclear language so I can help some poor soul Googling their little hearts out (seriously, tech bros, take a writing class).

M87
The star, far off in the sky, shone so brightly,
That I followed the light, shivering.

I gazed at my future self in the broken mirror,
I wanted to become strong, I idolized everything.

The wind blows, you look up at your hat, fluttering,
You go on a long, short journey,
A shadow of a distant day.

If you want it, you can get a definitive answer,
Don't be afraid of everything, know pain, just be.

The last words of the flower that is wilting now were
"Even if you can't see me, I'm watching over you from afar."

Yes, your heart has been crushed and whittled down,
The beginning of the story
Is a vague loneliness.

Your hands shook, if that was the power of loneliness paying off,
Who could steal it, and why? We need each other, until our lives end.

Beyond the trees' leaves, the glittering stars speak,
You just gaze up at them, thinking about the future,
We move forward, knowing nothing, into the great beyond.

If you want it, you can get a definitive answer,
Don't be afraid of everything, know pain, just be.

Smile faintly, like that star,
Know pain, just be.

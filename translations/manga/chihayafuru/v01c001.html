[Right Flap] Chihayafuru is a serialized work. I can't explain in words how fun and enjoyable it is to write a serialized manga. I hope you can understand through the manga itself. Let's start!
[Main Body] Chihaya, a sixth-grader, met Arata, a transfer student from Fukui. Arata was a quiet boy, but he had an unusual gift: competitive karuta. Chihaya felt inspired by Arata's ability to take cards faster and with more concentration than anyone else. However, what Arata couldn't take his eyes from was Chihaya's extraordinary talent.

The curtain finally raises on this story of youth, where ever more dazzling and wholehearted feelings intersect!!
